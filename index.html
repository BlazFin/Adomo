Why Choose Us?

At Zodiac Surveyors and Contractors, we specialize in expert damp surveying and a comprehensive range of remedial services. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every project we undertake meets the highest standards of quality and precision.Whether you need a damp proofing course, plastering finishes, or full maintenance contracting services, our experienced team is here to assist you.

    Specialized in damp surveying with extensive industry experience

  • Quality Assurance

    Delivering top-notch quality in every project

    Utilizing the latest technology and methods to ensure effective results

Services

Damp Surveying

Remedial Work
